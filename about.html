About SarkariAlert.in

India's trusted government job and result notification portal

About Us

SarkariAlert.in is India's dedicated government job and result notification portal. We collect, verify and publish all central and state government job notifications, exam results, admit cards, board results, syllabus and government schemes — so that every student and job seeker in India gets the right information at the right time.

Our mission is simple: no deserving candidate should miss a government job opportunity or board result because they didn't get the information in time.

We cover: Railway Jobs, UPSC, SSC, Banking, Defence, Police, State Government Jobs, Board Exam Results (CBSE, UP, Bihar, MP, Rajasthan etc.), University Results, Admit Cards, Syllabus and Government Schemes.

We do not collect personal data from visitors except when voluntarily submitted (e.g. email subscription). Email addresses are used only to send government job alerts and are never shared with third parties.
