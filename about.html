About Us

SarkariAlert.in is India's dedicated government job notification portal. We collect, verify, and publish all central and state government job notifications from official sources — so that every job seeker in India has the right information at the right time.

Our mission is simple: no deserving candidate should miss a government job opportunity because they didn't get the notification in time. We work every day to ensure the latest alerts reach you before anyone else.

What We Cover

We cover all types of government job notifications across India:

  • 🚂 Railway Jobs — RRB, Group D, ALP, NTPC, Apprentice and all zonal railways
  • 🏛️ UPSC Jobs — Civil Services (IAS/IPS), IFS, CDS, NDA, CAPF and all UPSC exams
  • 📝 SSC Jobs — CGL, CHSL, MTS, CPO, GD Constable and all SSC exams
  • 🏦 Banking Jobs — RBI, SBI, IBPS PO, IBPS Clerk, RRB and all bank jobs
  • 🛡️ Defence & Police — Army, Navy, Air Force, CRPF, BSF, CISF, SSB, State Police
  • 🗺️ State Government Jobs — All state PSCs, state police, teacher recruitment
  • 📮 Postal & PSU — India Post, Coal India, ONGC, BHEL and all PSU jobs
  • 🎓 Teaching Jobs — CTET, KVS, NVS, DSSSB and state teacher recruitment
